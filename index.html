Welcome to Hangar Database!

What is this site?

Here you will find a wide assortment of stunning pictures from the most important aircraft that made history. I dedicated this Blog/Gallery space for one of my biggest passions: self propelled flying machines! This does not only include planes, but Helicopters, Gyrocopters, Airships (or blimps as they are commonly referred to) as well.

From Piston to Jet powered aircraft, single engine biplanes to bombers powered by multiple egines, you will find a gallery dedicated to the beasts that conquered the air, with focus on military and historical aircraft.
